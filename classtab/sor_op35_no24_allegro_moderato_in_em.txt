Notes and Legend
----------------
"Le doigt qui fait la plus haute note doit la conserver tant qu'il n'est
pas oblige d'en faire une autre."
(Keep the high melody notes ringing as long as possible.)
The left hand fingering is from the sheet and is for an early 19th century
guitar (where the frets are closer together).
The tempo of the MIDI is 92 bpm (quarter notes).

left hand fingering below the tab -
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger
