Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
"La sonnambula (The Sleepwalker) is an opera semiseria in two acts, with music
in the bel canto tradition by Vincenzo Bellini set to an Italian libretto by
Felice Romani, based on a scenario for a ballet-pantomime written by Eugene
Scribe and choreographed by Jean-Pierre Aumer called La somnambule, ou L'arrivee
d'un nouveau seigneur. ...The first performance took place at the Teatro Carcano
in Milan on 6 March 1831." (Wikipedia)

Emilia Giuliani-Guglielmi's Op 3 was first published by Gio. Ricordi in 1836.

The DGA edition gives very little information about the fingering, so feel free
to experiment. The tab keeps mainly to the open strings and lower frets, but in
some places it may be better to use barres or positions higher up the guitar.

The tempo of the MIDI is 60 bpm (quarter notes). In the MIDI the melody is
allowed to ring out - the notes aren't dampened and are often held for longer
than shown in the sheet music. 

measure 12 - in the sheet the 1st note on the 4th beat is G# (not G)
