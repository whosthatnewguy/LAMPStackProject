Notes and Legend
----------------
"These short pieces are for the enjoyment of students with modest technical
 accomplishment. They are intended to emphasise the musicality of the instrument
 and to illutrate its many moods. [1. Air] The music should flow romantically
 and tenderly, the pauses being used to enhance the mood." - Ruth Nunn (1978)

h      - hammer-on
mf     - mezzoforte - moderately loud
mp     - mezzopiano - moderately loud
p      - (below the tab) piano - soft
p      - (between the notes) pull-off
pp     - pianissimo - very soft
rall.  - rallentando - slowing down (often becoming gradually softer)
