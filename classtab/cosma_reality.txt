Reality by Vladimir Cosma (1940-)
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Arranged for classical guitar by David Atkinson 
(kaum0@central.sussex.ac.uk) April 1995

First time round, play it without the notes in brackets, second time with them.
Or do what you think is best. Suggestions and criticisms welcome.

Roman numerals denote the fret position of the barres.
The numbers under the tab indicate the left hand fingering.

s = slide (makes transition easier on fretboard)

Try to allow the bass notes to run as long as possible.

Time sig is 4/4, key is G major.
