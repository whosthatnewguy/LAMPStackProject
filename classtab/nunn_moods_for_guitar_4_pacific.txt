Notes and Legend
----------------
"These short pieces are for the enjoyment of students with modest technical
 accomplishment. They are intended to emphasise the musicality of the
 instrument and to illutrate its many moods. [4. Pacific] The tune must
 predominate here. The open-string accompaniment should be very soft and
 the feeling of the swell of the ocean emphasised." - Ruth Nunn (1978)
