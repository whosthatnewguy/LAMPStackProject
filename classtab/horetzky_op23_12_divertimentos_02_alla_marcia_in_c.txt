Notes and Legend
----------------
The tempo of the Norbert Neunzling video is 120 bpm (eighth notes).
The tempo of the MIDI is 108 bpm (quarter notes).

measure 4 - in the sheet the 2nd C and the 2nd A are left as sharps (#) and
            are not naturalised
            in the Norbert Neunzling video, the chord on the 3rd beat isn't
            held and the 3rd finger is used for the next B on the 3rd string
            (which makes the pull-off easier to play) -

sf     - sforzando - forced, strongly accented, suddenly louder
