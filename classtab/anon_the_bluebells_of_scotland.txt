Notes & Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
"One of Scotland's favourite songs, 'The Bluebells of Scotland' was introduced
at Drury Lane Theatre in London, just after 1800, by the famous actress
Dorothea Jordan, nee Bland (1762-1816). This song was published by George
Thomson (1757-1851) who paid Joseph Hayden in Vienna two ducats for some 200
tunes, including Blue Bells of Scotland, to give the old folk tunes real class."
- Frank Perkins
