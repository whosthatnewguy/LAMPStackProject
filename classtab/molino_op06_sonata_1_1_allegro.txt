1 - index finger

/      - slide the note up
> > >  - decrescendo - becoming gradually softer
f      - forte - loud
g      - grace note - ornament - extra note(s) played at the same time as,
         or just before the main note, then immediately released
h      - hammer-on
M      - upper mordent (ornament) - play main note, followed by the note above
         (in the scale), and then return to original note - (may be extended)
p      - (below the tab) piano - soft
p      - (between the notes) pull-off
sf     - sforzando - suddenly loud
