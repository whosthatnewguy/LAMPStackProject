Notes and Legend
----------------
The tempo of the MIDI is 120 bpm (quarter notes).

left hand fingering below tab       right hand fingering above tab
1 - index finger                    p - thumb
2 - middle finger                   i - index finger
3 - ring finger                     m - middle finger
4 - little finger                   a - ring finger

===   - note held
>     - accented, marked, stressed
D.C.  - Da Capo - repeat from the start
f     - forte - loud
F.    - Fermata - pause
mf    - mezzoforte - moderately loud, medium loud

a tempo - return to the earlier tempo (after slowing down)
D.C. (ad libitum) - it is up to the performer whether to repeat the piece
(f e) sempre legato - (loud, and) continue to play smoothly to the end
poco rit. - poco ritenuto - a little slower, slowing down a little
