Notes and Legend
----------------
"The prelude is composed of a juxtaposition of a pesante theme (heavy and
ponderous) and a lighter quicker theme, the latter quoting a motif from the
first movement of Shostakovich's own 10th symphony." - Wikipedia

The Theo Radic edition (guitar) has time signature 4/4 throughout; where the
tab is in 3/2 (and 2/4) it follows the Moscow 1972 piano edition. The Theo
Radic edition differs from the piano edition in several places, especially
measures 39-43, and the left hand fingering doesn't always make sense!
The tempo of the MIDI file is 116 bpm (quarter notes).

measure 21 - the tab follows the piano edition (the notes in the Theo Radic
             edition don't add up to 4/4)
measures 27,28 - the Theo Radic edition has an open D (6th string) at the bottom
                 of the chord on the 1st beat, held throughout the measure
measure 28 - the chord on the 2nd beat in the Theo Radic edition (D-A-D-A-Bb) is
             unplayable - the full chord (piano edition) is A-A-D-A-Bb
measure 29 - the 8th note in the Theo Radic edition is C# (not Bb)
measure 35 - the G on the 2nd beat is a half note in both editions
measure 38 - the D (2nd string) on the 2nd beat is a half note in both editions

left hand fingering below the tab -
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger

mp     - (below the tab) mezzopiano - moderately soft
