Notes & Legend
--------------
There is no key signature, but the piece is similar to a 12-bar blues in E.
Much of the piece is written as 8 eighth notes per bar, but there is an
instruction to play each pair of eighth notes as a triplet made up of a quarter
note + an eighth note. Therefore the tab has been written in triplets.
The starting tempo of the MIDI is 116 bpm (quarter notes).

1 - index finger                    p - thumb

3 - ring finger                     m - middle finger
4 - little finger                   a - ring finger

====   - note held, length of note (duration)
|-3-|  - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes
/      - glissando - slide the note up
\      - glissando - slide the note down
F.     - Fermata - notes held for longer than their value
h      - (between the notes) hammer-on
p      - (between the notes) pull-off
rit.   - ritenuto - slowing down
