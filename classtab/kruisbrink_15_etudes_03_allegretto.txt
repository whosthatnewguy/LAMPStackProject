Notes & Legend
--------------
None of the Etudes in the set have a key signature. (Etude No 3 sounds close to
being in the key of A minor.) The tempo of the MIDI is 88 bpm (quarter notes).

left hand fingering below tab       right hand fingering above tab
1 - index finger                    p - thumb
2 - middle finger                   i - index finger
3 - ring finger                     m - middle finger
4 - little finger                   a - ring finger

===    - notes held, length of notes
,      - (measure 10) slight pause (breath mark)
|-3-|  - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes
< < <  - (below the tab) crescendo - growing louder
> > >  - (below the tab) decrescendo - growing softer
arp.   - arpeggiate - ripple upwards from the lowest note to the highest note
f      - forte - loud
F.     - Fermata
h      - hammer-on
mf     - mezzoforte - moderately loud
molto  - much, very - (molto rit. - slowing down a lot)
mp     - mezzopiano - moderately soft
p      - (below the tab) piano - soft
p      - (between the notes) - pull-off
poco   - a little - (poco rit. - slowing down a little)
rit.   - ritenuto - (suddenly) slowing down
s      - staccato - detached, cut off, the note is not held for its full value
tempo  - speed - (a tempo - return to the previous tempo, eg after rit.)
