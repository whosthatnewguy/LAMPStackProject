arp.  - ripple upwards from the lowest note to the highest note

F.    - Fermata - pause, note(s) held for longer than their value

       (in the scale), and then return to the original note - (may be extended)
p     - (between the notes) pull-off
rall. - rallentando - slowing down, getting slower
