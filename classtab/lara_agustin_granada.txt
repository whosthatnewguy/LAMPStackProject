The 5 and 6 string chords should be arpeggiated (ripple upwards from the
lowest note to the highest note), sweeping with the thumb. 

measure 72 - in the sheet the bottom note on the 1st beat is E (not E#)

3 - ring finger                     m - middle finger
4 - little finger                   a - ring finger
