Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Measure numbers are continued from Variation 3 so as to be consistent with
the sheeet music.

measure 105 - the sheet music does not have 'p' (piano) before the decrescendo

left hand fingering (below the tab)
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger

===      - duration, length of note
|-4-|    - quadruplet - 4 notes in the time of 3 notes
|-5-|    - pentuplet - 5 notes in the time of 3 notes
> > >    - (above the tab) decrescendo - growing softer
bpm      - beats per minute
c5       - part barre on the 5th fret
espress. - expressively, with expression, with feeling
p        - piano - soft
