Notes & Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
The order of play is different in the Yvonne Bloor reference audio -
order of play (tab): 1-29, 1-24, 30-35
order of play (audio): 6-29, 6-29, 35

left hand fingering below the tab
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger (pinky)

===   - note held, length of note
/     - (below the tab) slide the finger up to the next note
\     - (below the tab) slide the finger up down the next note
'     - (measure 18) breath mark, slight pause (luftpause)
+     - Coda sign (jump to the Coda - measure 30)
bpm   - beats per minute
Coda  - the end section (al Coda - continue to the Coda sign)
D.C.  - Da Capo - return to the start
F.    - Fermata - pause, note(s) held
h     - hammer-on
<7>   - open (natural) harmonic on the 7th fret

c1    - part barre on the 1st fret
C1    - barre on the 1st fret
c2    - part barre on the 2nd fret
C2    - barre on the 2nd fret
etc
