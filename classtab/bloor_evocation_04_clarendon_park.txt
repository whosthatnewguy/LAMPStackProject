Notes & Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

left hand fingering below tab       right hand fingering above tab
1 - index finger                    p - thumb
2 - middle finger                   i - index finger
3 - ring finger                     m - middle finger
4 - little finger                   a - ring finger

===   - note held, length of note
|-3-| - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes
/     - (below the tab) slide the finger up to the next note
'     - breath mark, slight pause (luftpause)
arp.  - ripple upwards from the lowest note to the highest note
bpm   - beats per minute
C3    - barre on the 3rd fret
c5    - part barre on the 5th fret
F.    - Fermata - pause, note(s) held
g     - grace note (ornament) - extra note(s) played immediately before (or
        at the same time as) the main note, then immediately released - here
        the grace notes are mainly played as a slide up to the main note
h     - hammer-on
M     - upper mordent (ornament) - play the main note, followed by the note
        above (in the scale), and then return to the original note
p     - (between the notes) pull-off
<7>   - open (natural) harmonic on the 7th fret
<12>  - open (natural) (octave) harmonic on the 12th fret
