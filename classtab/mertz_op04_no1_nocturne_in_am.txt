Notes and Legend

The tempo of the MIDI is 54 bpm (beats per minute) (quarter notes).

measures 20 - (similarly measures 21,22) the timing of the sixteenth notes -

              |---3---|   |---3---|
              |   .   .   |   .   .
             -------------------------|
             -------------------------|
             -1===========----2-------|
             -----3-3-3---3-------3---|
             -3=======3---3---3---3---|
             -------------------------|

measure 28 - in the sheet this is the same as measure 5 except that the last
             2 notes are not staccato (?)

left hand fingering below the tab -
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger (pinky)

===     - note held
>       - accented, marked, stressed
|-3-|   - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes
< < <   - crescendo - becoming gradually louder
c1      - part barre on the 1st fret
c3      - part barre on the 3rd fret
f       - forte - loud
ff      - fortissimo - very loud
F.      - Fermata - note(s) held, pause
h       - hammer-on
p       - (between the notes) pull-off
p       - (below the tab) piano - soft
pp      - pianissimo - very soft
ritard. - ritardando - slowing down
s       - staccato - detached, cut off, the note is not held for its full value
sf      - sforzando - forced, strongly accented, suddenly louder
tempo   - speed (a tempo - return to the earlier tempo)
