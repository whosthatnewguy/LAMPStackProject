Notes and Legend
----------------
"I Capuleti e i Montecchi (The Capulets and the Montagues) is an Italian
opera (Tragedia lirica) in two acts by Vincenzo Bellini. The libretto by
Felice Romani was a reworking of the story of Romeo and Juliet for an opera
by Nicola Vaccai called Giulietta e Romeo and is based on the play of the
same name by Luigi Scevola written in 1818, thus an Italian source rather
than taken directly from William Shakespeare." (Wikipedia)
Emilia Giuliani-Guglielmi's Op 1 was first published by Gio. Ricordi in 1834.
The left hand fingering below the tab is from the sheet music, but the strings
to be used aren't shown and there are choices as to which position to use.
The tempo of the MIDI is 80 bpm (quarter notes).
