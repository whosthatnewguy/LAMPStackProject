Notes and Legend
----------------
The Benjamin Dameron video is based on the Tristan Manoukian arrangement,
but in some passages different fingering is used.
The MIDI tempo is 32 bpm (quarter notes) for the Grave (measures 1-7),
40 bpm for the Andante (m 8-29), and 80 bpm for the Fugue (m 30-91).

measure 14 - in the sheet the Bb on the 2nd beat is an 1/8th note
measure 28 - in the sheet the D#-A-F# on the first beat are quarter notes,
             and the E-E-A on the 3rd beat are half notes
measure 29 - in the sheet the E on the 1st beat is a whole note, and the D#
             on the 1st beat is a quarter note tied to a dotted quarter note!
measure 30 - in the sheet the note on the 2nd beat is C (not C#)
measure 51 - in the sheet the F on the 1st beat is a quarter note
