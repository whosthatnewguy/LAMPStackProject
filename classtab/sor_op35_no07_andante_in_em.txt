Notes and Legend
----------------
The left hand fingering is mainly taken from the Simrock 2768 edition.
The tempo of the MIDI is 72 bpm (dotted quarter notes).

left hand fingering below tab -
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger

===  - note held
/    - (between the notes) slide the note up
g     - grace note - acciaccatura - played as a pull-off immediately before or
        at the same time as the following note, and immediately released
h    - hammer-on
CII  - barre on the 2nd fret
cIII - part barre on the 3rd fret
CIII - barre on the 3rd fret
