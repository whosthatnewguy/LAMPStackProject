Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
David Kellner was a German lute composer of the baroque period.
Hubert Kappel has changed the original lute music in measures 9,10,34,43,55.
MIDI tempo: Allegro 88 bpm (quarter notes), Adagio (1) 60 bpm (quarter notes),
            Presto assai 180 bpm (quarter notes), Adagio (2) 28 bpm (half notes)

measure 8 - in the sheet the notes of the 1st chord (B-D#-F#)are half notes,
            but this makes it difficult to play the G (6th string 5th fret)

measure 37 - the tab follows the sheet, but this fingering may be easier -

             35 |   .   |   .   |   .
              |-------------------------|
              |---------------------0---|
              |---------5---------------|
              |-----4-------4-----------|
              |-5===============5=======|
              |-------------------------|

measure 48: the last 2 notes are a dotted eighth note and a sixteenth note 

measure 50 - in the sheet the bass note on the 2nd beat is G (not F) - (all the
             reference recordings except the one by Aniko Juhasz play F)

left hand fingering below tab       right hand fingering above tab
1 - index finger                    p - thumb
2 - middle finger                   i - index finger
3 - ring finger                     m - middle finger
4 - little finger (pinky)           a - ring finger

===  - length of note, note held, duration
=-=  - let the note ring, don't dampen the sound
bpm  - beats per minute
F.   - Fermata - pause, note(s) held
h    - hammer-on
p    - (between the notes) pull-off

c1   - part barre on the 1st fret
C1   - barre on the 1st fret
c2   - part barre on the 2nd fret
C2   - barre on the 2nd fret
etc
