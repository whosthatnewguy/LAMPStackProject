Notes and Legend
----------------
The tempo of the MIDI is 30 bpm (dotted quarter notes).
The edition revised by Carlo Carfagna (Berben, Ancora, 1989) has a repeat sign
at the end of measure 12.

measure 2 - Paolo Amico and Rachel Schiff both play the 16th note (A) on the
            10th fret of the 2nd string (which is probably smoother) but the
            sheet shows a hammer-on from the A to the C

measure 4 - the 1st 2 notes on the 2nd beat (E 3rd string, E 1st string) are
            both eighth notes in the Berben edition (so the 2nd E is played
            with the E on the 6th string)

left hand fingering below the tab -
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger

===  - note held, length of note, duration
h    - hammer-on
p    - (between the notes) pull-off
p    - (below the tab) piano - soft
sf   - sforzando - forced, strongly accented, suddenly louder
