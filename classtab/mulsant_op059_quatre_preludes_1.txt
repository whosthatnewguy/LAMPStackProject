Notes and Legend
----------------
"The work with a duration of 10 minutes has been written during autumn 2016.
It is commissioned by the Festival d'Ouessant and is dedicated to the guitarist
Caroline Delume. The first prelude has a melodic character and opens the cycle
in a soft and luminous atmosphere." (from the sheet)

There are some difficult stretches. The sheet doesn't give any fingering -
suggestions for improving the fingering in the tab are welcome.

measure 5 - the F on the 2nd beat could be played on the 2nd string 6th fret
measure 11 - the 7th note (A) could also be played on the 2nd string 10th fret

===    - notes held, length of note, duration
>      - accented, marked, stressed
< < <  - crescendo - becoming gradually louder
> > >  - decrescendo - becoming gradually softer
arp.   - arpeggiate - ripple upwards from the lowest note to the highest note
bpm    - beats per minute
f      - forte - loud
ff     - fortissimo - very loud
F.     - Fermata - note(s) held, (long) pause
Lent   - slow
mf     - mezzoforte - moderately loud
mp     - mezzopiano - moderately soft
p      - (below the tab) piano - soft
Plus   - more (Plus lent - slower)
rall.  - rallentando - slowing down (often becoming gradually softer)
t      - (above the tab) tenuto - played firmly, slightly accented -
         note held for at least its full length, or a little longer
tempo  - speed (A tempo - return to the earlier tempo)
