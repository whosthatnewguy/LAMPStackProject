left hand fingering below the tab -
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger

===   - note held, length of note, duration
/     - slide the note up
$     - (end of measure 21) segno - sign
bpm   - beats per minute
D.C.  - go back to the start
F.    - (measure 57) Fermata - pause (before starting the next Etude) 
g     - grace note (ornament) - extra note(s) played immediately before (or
        at the same time as) the main note, then immediately released - the
        following note is usually played as a hammer-on, pull-off, or slide
h     - hammer-on
p     - (between the notes) pull-off
rit.  - ritenuto - slowing down, suddenly slower
segno - sign (al segno - play until the sign)
tempo - speed (a tempo - return to the earlier speed)
tr.   - trill (ornament) - a rapid alternation of a note with the note above it
        in the scale (usually starting on the note above)
<12>  - (measure 38) natural (open) (octave) harmonic on the 12th fret
