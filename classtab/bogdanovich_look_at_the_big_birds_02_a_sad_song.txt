Notes and Legend
----------------
The tempo of the saitenspuren video and the MIDI is 90 bpm (quarter notes).

left hand fingering below the tab -
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger

===   - note held, length of note, duration
>     - accented, marked , stressed
,     - short pause (breath mark) (at the end of measure 13)
|-3-| - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes
< < < - crescendo - becoming gradually louder
> > > - decrescendo - getting softer, becoming gradually quieter
bpm   - beats per minute
C1    - barre on the 1st fret
C2    - barre on the 2nd fret
F.    - Fermata - note(s) held, pause
h     - hammer-on
M     - upper mordent (ornament) - play main note, followed by the note above
        (in the scale), and then return to original note - (may be extended)
mf    - mezzo forte - moderately loud
p     - (below the tab) piano - soft, quietly
p     - (between the notes) pull-off
rit.  - ritenuto - slowing down, suddenly slower
tr    - trill (ornament) - fast alternation of a note with the scale note above
