Notes and Legend
----------------
The tempo of the Susan McDonald videos is ~100 bpm.
The tempo of the MIDI is 92 bpm.

measure 13 - in the sheet the 2nd and 3rd notes are a major 3rd lower
             (E-E-G#-E, not E-G#-B-E, as in measure 3), which sounds ok,
             but i think in the Susan McDonlad video it is E-G#-B-E

measures 19,20 - in the sheet the open A on the 5th string is a quarter note -
                 (not a sixteenth note) - (the tab follows the video)
