Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
left hand fingering below the tab -
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3  ring finger
4 - little finger

===   - note held, length of note, duration
/     - slide the note up
>     - accented, marked, stressed
'     - (measure 16) breath mark, slight pause
bpm   - beats per minute
c5    - part barre on the 5th fret
F.    - Fermata - note(s) held, pause
g     - grace note (ornament) - extra note played before the main note,
p     - pull-off
<12>  - open (natural) (octave) harmonic on the 12th fret
2<14> - artificial harmonic, left hand 2nd fret, right hand points to 14th fret
