Notes and Legend
----------------
The piece is divided into sections by broken bar lines, which are interpreted
as measures. The only solid bar lines are at the end of measures 7,25,26,50,52.

Much of the piece is atonal and some 'measures' are very long, so there are
many accidentals (sharps, flats and natural signs), some of which appear to
hold throughout the measure and others not, which means a certain amount of
guesswork is needed to decide what the notes are. Because of this ambiguity,
there are probably errors in the tab (and perhaps the sheet). Corrections
to either the tab or the accompanying MIDI file are welcome.
