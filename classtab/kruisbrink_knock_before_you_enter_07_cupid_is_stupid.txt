Notes & Legend
--------------

1 - index finger                    p - thumb

====   - note held, length of note (duration)
>      - accented, stressed
|-3-|  - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes
F.     - Fermata - pause, note(s) held

a tempo - resume the original tempo (return to playing at the earlier speed)
poco rit. - poco ritenuto - a little slower
rit. - ritenuto, slowing down, suddenly slower
