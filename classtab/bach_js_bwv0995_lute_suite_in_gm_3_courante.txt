Notes and Legend
----------------
editions: Eva Jaksch - Werner Icking Music Archive, 2002
          Edmund Wensiecki - Friedrich Hofmeister FH 4035, Leipzig, 1965
          Editio Musica Budapest Z8309 (Hungary)
          Eythor Thorlaksson - The Guitar School 1125, Iceland
          Jean Francois Delcamp - delcamp.net (Paris)

Ornaments are played variously as grace notes (acciaccaturas and appogiaturas),
mordents, and trills in different editions of the sheet music. Similarly many
editions include hammer-ons and pull-offs. Some editions prefer high frets,
while other editions prefer a greater use of lower frets and open strings.
An example of the latter is the 1st chord of measure 6, where the E on the 5th
string 7th fret is sometimes played an octave lower on the open 6th string.

The MIDI tempo is 62 bpm (half notes).
