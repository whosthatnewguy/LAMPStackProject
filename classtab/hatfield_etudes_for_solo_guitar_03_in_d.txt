Notes & Legend
--------------
The tab is based on the Rob Mackillop video. In the Andrew Whelan and the Rob
Mackillop videos, the guitar is tuned one semitone lower - Eb G# C# F# Bb Eb.

===   - note held
p     - pull-off
h     - hammer-on
3<22> - art harmonic, left hand 3rd fret, right hand points to 22nd fret
<7>   - Natural harmonic on the 7th fret
