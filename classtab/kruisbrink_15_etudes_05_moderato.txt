Notes & Legend
--------------
None of the Etudes in the set have a key signature.
The tempo of the MIDI is 120 bpm (quarter notes).

measure 8 - arpeggiate the grace notes, ripple from the 3rd to the 1st string

left hand fingering below tab       right hand fingering above tab
1 - index finger                    p - thumb
2 - middle finger                   i - index finger
3 - ring finger                     m - middle finger
4 - little finger                   a - ring finger

===   - notes held, length of notes
|-3-| - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes
/     - (below the tab) slide the finger up to the next note
\     - (below the tab) slide the finger down to the next note
/     - (between the notes) slide the note up
arp   - arpeggiate - ripple upwards from the lowest note to the highest note
bpm   - beats per minute
c2    - part barre on the 2nd fret
dolce - sweetly
g     - grace note (ornament) - extra note played just before the main note
h     - hammer on
l.v.  - laissez vibrer (let vibrate) - let it ring, don't dampen the sound
p     - (below the tab) piano - soft
