Maxime Bergeron's scrolling app
2800 classical guitar tabs in plain text format arranged alphabetically by composer. This page is primarily for classical music played on a standard nylon acoustic classical guitar.
Tabs of self-composed and unpublished pieces should be submitted to  TIP-UOUCG.

The scrolling app should work on desktops, laptops, tablets and mobiles. The app window allows a tab to be scrolled horizontally across the screen at a speed selected by the user. The app now works with most of the single version tabs (though not for duets). Use the "Return to Classtab" link (top left of the app window) to return to the tabs page rather than your browser's back arrow, which may not work as expected.

A 9MB downloadable zip file (updated monthly) is available of the whole site including all the tabs and MIDI files. To restore the page on your own system, download the zip file, unzip it into an empty folder, and then open the file 'index.htm' with your web browser.
You can then Bookmark (Favorite) it in your web browser, as with any other web page.
